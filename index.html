The Starting Point

AI arrived. Everyone had ideas.

AI hit the organization and suddenly every business unit and function had ideas. New use cases, new tools, new ambitions — all at once. No central place. No coordination. Ideas popping up everywhere.

How do you prevent silos? How do you stop shadow IT? We needed to know what was happening. Systematically.
